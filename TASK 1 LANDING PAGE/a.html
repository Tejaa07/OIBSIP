Favourite Places

Baga Beach

Baga Beach is quite famous for its commercial nightlife. The famous Tito's Lane has many bars, lounges, and clubs like Club Tito, Cafe Mambo, Capetown Cafe, Cocktails and Dreams.

Candolim Beach

Nonetheless the Candolim Beach is also famous for hosting India's biggest electronic music dance festival, the Sunburn Festival, which is organized by Percept.

St. Alex Church

Divine Place A very beautiful church and a must-visit place to spend some time in worship of god. A good place to click pictures and get to know the culture of goa.

Palolem Island Reserve

This unique biodiversity hot-spot off the South Goa coast is a collaborative effort comprised of local fishermen & conservationists.
